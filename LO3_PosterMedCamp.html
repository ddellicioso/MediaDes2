Media Campaign Poster

Description

My teammates and I discussed about what products we should make for this project and we came up with several products, and one of them is posters. Me and one of my teammates are assigned to make a poster about one of the events held by Fontys which is Innovation Insights.

What I Did

First of all we sketched some ideas for the layout and the structure of the content. After having a rough idea on what the layout should look like, then we had a small brainstorming session. Since the event was about innovation and insights, we thought putting a light bulb would be fitting to the theme.

I drew the defined sketch to see which colors from Fontys' brand guide fit with the idea that I had. After I liked the color, I moved to Adobe Illustrator to make the poster/illustration. I used Fontys' main color (purple) as the background color and pink as a secondary color.

Feedback

After finishing the poster we came up to a teacher and asked for their opinion about it:

  • The color fits the brand guide
  • Good illustration
  • Should put the "triangle" from the brand guide since Fontys paid a lot for it

Iteration

After the feedback, we directly implemented the "triangle" into our poster. Since we didn't find the ready-made triangle online, we had to make it ourselves.
To match the overall colors and vibes we used pink for the triangle, and we also put more contents in it.

Reflection

How it went: Making the poster for the Innovative Insights event at Fontys was a collaborative and creative experiment. My teammates and I started by discussing various product ideas, and one of them was creating a poster. The process began with brainstorming and sketching out different layouts and content structures. Our initial designs were refined based on the feedback we received from our teacher, which then resulted in the final poster. The journey overall was smooth, and we were able to effectively use the elements of Fontys' brand guide, including the specific colors and the triangle motif.

What I learned: I learned the importance of effective teamwork and communication, and working closely with my teammate made us able to share ideas and refine our concepts together. Sticking to Fontys' brand guide was necessary for maintaining consistency in design to align with brand identity.

What will change: I would start with more research on the event and similar design projects. Understanding the context and previous designs could inspire more innovative designs. I also would try to implement more structured brainstorming techniques, such as mind mapping or analysis, which could help generate a wider range of ideas and solutions.
