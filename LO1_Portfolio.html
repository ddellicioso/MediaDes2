Portfolio Prototype

Description

I made both low fidelity and high fidelity prototype for my portfolio so that I can test my ideas, gather some feedbacks and iterate my designs before start to implement them

What I Did

After making a quick sketch about the layout and everything, I directly went to make a low fidelity prototype in Figma to make a quick iterations and to see if I need to change or add anything. when I'm sure that it's good enough, I made the high fidelity.

When I was done I went to 5 different teachers to ask about their opinions about it and to do user testing on them. So far when I showed them, they didn't have any problem with it (with any display, colors, or functionality) and majority of them had a very positive feedback about my idea.


Feedback

One of the teacher, Anke, said that I should change the pop-up layout for the content because she's afraid that the pop-up will be too long and it'll be boring. After hearing about this, I try to think about something else that is not a pop-up, so I thought that maybe I should make a whole new page for the contents. Unfortunately I didn't make the new layout into a figma prototype.

I showed my idea directly to Anke and she said that it's really nice and she likes the idea of the slideshow.

Reflection

I'm really happy that a lot of teachers approved my idea and had very positive vision about it.
