Call-Out Button Implementation

Description

During a lecture emphasizing the importance of using call-out buttons, I proposed implementing one in our Fontys Website project. After discussing with my teammates, they agreed to incorporate the call-out button on the homepage.

What I Did

I began by researching tutorials on W3Schools and examining the call-out button on the real Fontys website for reference. Utilizing the brand guide colors (pink and purple) and fonts, I designed the call-out button to be sticky in the same spot. However, I ensured users could dismiss it by clicking the X button. To draw attention, I made the button large and clickable, directing users to the admission page when clicked.

Feedback

On our last presentation day, we showed our product to our client and Anke:

  • The client expressed appreciation for the sticky position and agreed it encouraged clicks.
  • During user testing, all participants clicked the call-out button, indicating its effectiveness

Reflection

How it went: Incorporating the call-out button proved successful, garnering positive feedback from both the client and user testing.

What I learned: This experience reinforced the importance of considering user attention and interaction design principles. It also highlighted the effectiveness of using brand colors and fonts for consistency.

What will change: In future projects, I will continue to use design elements like call-out buttons to enhance user engagement. I'll also prioritize user testing to validate design decisions and ensure optimal user experience.
