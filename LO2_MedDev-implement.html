Project 3 Website Development

Description

For the third and last group project, we were asked to fix fontys ICT’s website and implement them. My teammates and I made some iterations of Figma prototype of our ideas on our version of fontys website before actually implementing them.

What I Did

I was tasked to make the profile page (media page), so after receiving answers from survey and did some iterations regarding the page, I started making the HTML CSS. Fortunately, my teammates already made the navigation and footer section, so I just needed to focu on making the profile page.

The responses from the survey really helped a lot because I didn’t need to think how I should organize the content since the respondents already told me what to do as well as which design I should make.

The hardest part to make was making the carousel image for the job opportunity section. I did make something similar for my portfolio (previously) but the mechanic is a little bit different. Instead of showing the images one by one, I needed to show 3 images at once and when next/previous button is clicked, the carousel moves only the most left/right image. After the struggle, I finally succeeded to make it functional even though it’s a little bit different with the prototype but I did my best.
