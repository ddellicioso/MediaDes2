Eindhoven League

Description

The Eindhoven League, organized by PPI Eindhoven, is a sports tournament hosted by the Indonesian Student Association in Eindhoven.

PPI Eindhoven, short for "Perhimpunan Pelajar Indonesia Eindhoven" or the "Indonesian Student Association in Eindhoven", operates within a global network of Indonesian student associations across different cities. It provides a community and support system for Indonesian students studying in Eindhoven.

What I Did

My involvement in the Eindhoven League, an annual sports competition organized by Indonesian students in Eindhoven, began as a personal project. In this capacity, I assumed the role of Social Media coordinator, responsible for creating and posting content (both feed and Instagram stories) on the PPI Eindhoven Instagram account. To craft visually engaging posts, I utilized Adobe Illustrator for illustration-based content and Canva for adding textual elements.

Reflection

How it went: My journey with the Eindhoven League project was both rewarding and enlightening. As the Social Media coordinator, I encountered various challenges and learned valuable lessons along the way. Initially, navigating platforms like Adobe Illustrator and Canva posed some difficulties, but through perseverance and online tutorials, I quickly grasped the fundamentals of graphic design.

What I learned: I learned how to manage my time better because this personal project stole quite a lot of my school time. I learned the importance of effective communication and visual storytelling in social media management. Moreover, collaborating with team members and receiving feedback allowed me to refine my skills and explore new approaches to content creation.

How it went: Moving forward, I intend to apply the lessons learned from this project to future endeavors. I plan to continue improving my skills in graphic design and social media management, seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and explore innovative techniques. Additionally, I will prioritize clear communication and collaboration within teams, recognizing the value of collective efforts in achieving successful outcomes.
