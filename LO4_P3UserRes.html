Project 3 : User Research

Description

This report summarizes the findings of a user survey conducted to gather feedback on the Fontys website, specifically focusing on the ICT program. Participants provided insights on design preferences, content importance, and user experience, highlighting areas for improvement to enhance the website's appeal and usability.

What I Did

I made the survey by putting together a bunch of questions to find out what people really thought about the Fontys website, especially the ICT programs part. After spreading the survey and getting diverse bunch of students, I started sorting through them. I spotted some patterns and cool insights that I turned into practical suggestions. I pitched these ideas to my teammates and we started to make our products.
With the survey in hand, I made a comprehensive report summarizing key insights and participant feedback. I used this report as a foundation to iterate my prototype which later on will be implemented in the website.

Reflection

How it went: The survey yielded valuable insights into user preferences and areas for improvement on the Fontys website. Participants provided constructive feedback on design elements, content sections, and overall user experience.

What I learned: I learned bout participants' preferences for design elements, including the use of purple and bordered elements, while also noting suggestions for improving readability and visual appeal. I also gained insights into user expectations for a user-friendly layout that avoids information overload and maintains a balance between text and images.

What will change: I'd adopt a user-centered design approach to create an engaging and user-friendly website that effectively communicates the benefits and details of Fontys' ICT programs.
