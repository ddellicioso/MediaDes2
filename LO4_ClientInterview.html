Project 1 : Client Interview

Description

We came to Geldrop once again to interview our clients to know and understand better what they actually wants because from the first presentation wasn’t really much of a help.

What I Did

I coordinated and conducted an interview with Marianne and Cees to gain deeper insights into the orchestra's challenges and needs. During the interview, I asked questions about their membership trends, reasons for the decline, recruitment efforts, and the tools they use for their website and social media presence. I also explored their familiarity with different platforms and their openness to learning new technologies.

Reflection

How it went: Conducting the interview provided valuable insights into the orchestra's situation. It was clear that while they have a stable base of elderly members, attracting younger members remains a significant challenge. The interview revealed specific areas where they need support, such as improving their website and enhancing their social media presence.

What I learned: I learned the importance of making recruitment strategies for different age groups. For example, while workshops in schools may not yield immediate results, targeting elderly individuals through community events has been more successful. I also realized the necessity of being familiar with and effectively using digital tools like Wix and social media platforms to reach broader audiences.

What will change: Before the interview, I would research best practices for recruiting young members to orchestras and successful case studies from other organizations. I would also implement more structured interview techniques, such as a clear list of questions and follow-up prompts.
