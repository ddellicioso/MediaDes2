Project Branding Prototype

Description

For this learning outcome we made a Figma prototype to implement our ideas for the client's website. Before making this website, we specifically interviewed our clients(Marianne and Cees) and made surveys for our target audience (*more details in Learning Outcome *)

What I Did

After we made sure about what our clients and target audience wants, I was assigned to make several pages such as, Agenda page, About Us page, New Talents page, and Percussion page. First of all I skimmed through the GMC's old website to see where the problem is, which part need fixings, while also discussing with my teammates.

I also made sure to use the same colors and match the theme like our brand guide as well as my teammates' designs.

The part that I really liked was about "New Talent" page. I thought that this page has a potential (the left side is the original GMC web and right side is my idea),
so I put more things in it for example the group lessons details (which I think they didn't advertise them much and they should do) along with some informations, a photo gallery about people who joined them, and a simple FAQ.


Feedback

We didn't have time to do user testing nor ask for some feedback from anyone before the presentation. On the day of the final presentation, our clients said:

  • Agenda Page looks old fashioned
  • They like the fonts we used
  • They like the program details in the New Talents Page

Unfortunately since the feedback are given on the last presentation, I couldn't change anything about it. The reason why I made it like that is that because we think that our client will be easier to update or change it because they already know how to use it. But nonetheless, I do agree with their comments.

Reflection

How did it went: From my view, I did everything I could do and was assigned to. Though I feel unfortunate about the Agenda page.

What did I learn: I couldn't get any feedback because I didn't manage my time properly and I ran out of time . So I realized that managing my time and schedule is important especially with the short amount of time given to do this project.

What will change: I want to make sure that I do my best to have enough time to ask for a feedback before making a product, I also should do deeper research and user testing about the prototype to make it even better
