Groups Files Organizing

Description

For overall management of our files, all of my groups (project 1, 2, and 3) used the same apps. For every group project, I made sure to make a new group in Teams so that we can compile our files that we made for the group projects in there nicely and everyone in the group can access it.

What I Did

These are the apps that we used to organize our work as a team:

  1. Microsoft Teams:
    • We used it as a place to upload and organize our files
    • We were able to simultaneously edit our documents together (e.g., Word, PowerPoint, etc.)
    • We used it for project management such as using Planner to create tasks and assign them to team members
  2. WhatsApp:
    • We always made a group chat so we can communicate easily
    • When needed, we would arrange voice calls to discuss when some members couldn't join us in school

Reflection

How it went: Managing the files and communications for our group projects using Microsoft Teams and WhatsApp was effective and streamlined our collaborative efforts. Setting up new groups in Teams for each project allowed us to compile and organize our files efficiently. Using Teams for real-time document editing and project management tasks enhanced our productivity. Additionally, WhatsApp provided a convenient platform for quick communication and voice calls when necessary, ensuring that everyone stayed updated and involved.

What I learned: I learned the importance of having a centralized location for all project-related files. This practice ensured that every team member had access to the latest versions of documents, which minimized confusion and streamlined our workflow.

What will change: I would implement more structured planning sessions at the beginning of the project to outline our goals, deadlines, and individual responsibilities more clearly. This would provide a stronger foundation for our collaboration. I also would explore additional features within Ms Teams, such as integrated apps and more advanced project management tools, to further enhance our workflow and efficiency.
