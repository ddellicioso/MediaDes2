Project Media Campaign Research

Description

We made several surveys for ICT students in Fontys to understand where people heard about Fontys, and thus we made a respective product based on their answers.

What I Did

I suggested myself to be a marketing team because I wanted to fullfil the learning outcome. Me and other my teammate decided to make a "Success Video" by interviewing an alumni that has graduated from Fontys and is working. First we started listing down some questions that would make new students interested in joining Fontys , for example their experiences, their ups downs studying at Fontys, and some tips and tricks.

A trouble that came in our way is that we didn't know anyone who met our criteria (an alumni from ICT Media Design, graduated some time ago, is working), so we decided to ask for some help from one of our teacher, Anke.
