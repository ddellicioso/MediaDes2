Eindhoven League Iterations

Description

Eindhoven League is organized by PPI Eindhoven and refers to a sports tournament hosted by the Indonesian Student Association in Eindhoven.

PPI Eindhoven stands for "Perhimpunan Pelajar Indonesia Eindhoven", which translates to "Indonesian Student Association in Eindhoven". This organization is part of a network of Indonesian student associations in various cities around the world. PPI Eindhoven serves as a community and support system for Indonesian students studying at institutions in Eindhoven.

What I Did

I joined Eindhoven League (a yearly sport competition held by Indonesian Students in Eindhoven) as a personal project. Here I was assigned to be the Social Media person and I was tasked to make the posts (feed and Instagram story) to be uploaded in PPI Eindhoven Instagram account. I used Adobe Illustrator to make an illustration-only post and Canva to put contents inside the posts.

The first task I received was to make a color palette and logo. I looked into Pinterest for references and inspirations for both. This year's theme is "Gear Up: Innovate and Win" and I felt like sci-fi or cyberpunk colors would fit with the theme, so I combined these inspirations and asked our team (Social Media team) to vote which one fits more with the theme.

Most of the people voted for the second palette, so we used that as our color scheme.
Next was to make the logo for this year's competition. The theme for this year is "Gear Up" so I thought that something with gears would fit the theme. I sketched several logo ideas and asked my peers for feedback.

Feedback 1

  • Top 3 most voted logos after the voting was conducted
  • People didn't like too much text on the logo
  • Top 1 and 2 were concerning due to their width

Iteration 1

After further discussion, we decided to use the 3rd logo (3 gears) because it represents both sports and gears (the theme). After deciding on the logo, I moved to Illustrator to illustrate it. To be honest, this was my first time using Adobe Illustrator, so I struggled quite a lot to figure out how to implement it.
*See details on how I made the logo in LO5*.

What I Did (making the poster)

After successfully making the color palette and the logo, I moved on to making the poster to be posted on the Instagram account. I needed to make a carousel "coming soon" post on Instagram.

I used the color palette and added a touch of futuristic theme. For example, I made a hologram projector to "project" the words, and used glow effects on most elements to enhance the futuristic feel. I had the hardest time making the gears since this was my first time using Adobe Illustrator.
But in the end, I succeeded in making the post and it was directly posted on Instagram. *Check the post here!*

Reflection

How it went: Initially, I joined Eindhoven League as a personal project, focusing on social media posts for PPI Eindhoven. My tasks included creating a color palette and logo, and designing a poster for Instagram. The process involved multiple iterations and feedback sessions, which significantly improved the final output. Working with Illustrator for the first time was challenging, but ultimately rewarding.
Dividing my time for this personal project and school's projects are quite hard for me since I needed to do lots of things and sometimes I needed to sacrifice my time for this projects making me not putting much attention and details into it.

What I learned: Through this project, I learned the importance of clear, structured content and effective design principles. I also gained valuable experience using Adobe Illustrator, enhancing my technical skills and ability to translate conceptual ideas into visual designs.

What will change: In future projects, I want to learn more about things I could do to improvise and enhance the products that I made and posted. I also want to make sure that my produts are professional enough to be showcased to people.
