Project Media Development

Description

We already made a figma prototype from the previous project (I wasn't in the prototype team) so we just extracted what we liked and agreed on. After that we made a new Figma prototype to have a general idea and themes/colors to match Fontys' brandguide. The next step is to make a user *put a link* research survey or A/B Testing focused on our target audience (Dutch teenagers) to see which designs or contents they like/want to see more.

What I Did

My part is to make the profile page (Media Design). In the survey, I asked the respondents to pick which design and colors they like the most as well as asking them about how they think the content should be arranged like.

I made several iterations on the page (ver1 is what we (our team) like, ver2 what teachers think should be, ver3/final is the modified version according to the answers of our target audience) *more detailed explanation in LO**.

Feedback

We came to Jan to ask for some feedback for the prototype (Ver 1) and he said that we should shorten the length of our pages to reduce the time of user scrolling.
The other thing is that he feels that the contact us section (in Ver 1) is too out of place and overall is just not a good color for the page. He also thinks that the pink header (we showed him the landing page as well) is nice and it fits the brandguide and the theme.

Iteration 1

After Jan's feedback I directly changed the things he mentioned such as, the header and the contact section. I also took away the additional section to shorten the page a little bit and I think it's not really important to put it in the profile page.

Iteration 2

After we got answers from our survey, I compiled everything and fix whatever is needed. I fixed the arrangement of the content, I changed the designs of the buttons and the job opportunity. I also separated the example project from the semester planning.

Reflection

Overall I feel like I did a quite goodjob because I took an account for every feedbacks that I received into my product and I'm really satisfied and happy with end product :D
