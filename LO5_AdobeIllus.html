Learning Adobe Illustrator

Description

The first time I used Illustrator is when I started joining Eindhoven League in December 2023 as a social media person. So I was very much needed to start learning Illustrator to illustrate logo and posters *see in here

What I Did

The first thing I needed to make was a logo for Eindhoven League, and it was something with gears and sports balls (soccer ball, volley ball, basket ball). I was very confused at first so I decided to go to YouTube to look for some tutorials how to make gears and those sport balls.

I also needed to put some neon effects on both logo and poster, which turns out after I watched the tutorials, I only need to copy paste Gaussian Blur for couple of times.

The hardest part for me is that making the glitch effect in the logo. If u see properly, there are some distortion in it and it makes my laptop dead for couple of times and I had to repeat it several times until I almost given up on it.

For the glitch part in the poster, I only cut the words horizontally into 3 parts and I just moved it a little bit so it can mimick the glitch movement.


Reflection

How it went: My journey with Illustrator kicked off when I joined the Eindhoven League in December 2023. While initially daunting, I found my footing through YouTube tutorials, which proved invaluable in navigating Illustrator's features. Despite encountering technical hurdles, such as laptop crashes during glitch effect implementation, I persevered and successfully completed the tasks at hand.

What I learned: Through this experience, I gained proficiency in Illustrator, mastering techniques like applying neon effects and creating glitch effects. Moreover, I cultivated resilience in the face of challenges, learning to troubleshoot effectively and persist until achieving desired outcomes. This hands-on experience significantly enhanced my design skills and confidence in utilizing design software.

What will change: Moving forward, I aim to approach projects with a more strategic mindset, allocating adequate time for troubleshooting and seeking assistance when needed. Additionally, I plan to further explore Illustrator's capabilities through diverse learning resources to enhance my proficiency. By embracing challenges as opportunities for growth and maintaining a proactive attitude, I look forward to continuing my journey in graphic design.
