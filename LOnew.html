Project Branding

These are the Figma prototype for GMC website that I made.
We didn’t have any much time left to ask for feedback so we designed them by thinking what would our client and people wants;
I don’t really like how the agenda page looks like because it looked like an old website, but after talking to one of our teammates, she thinks that it’ll be easier for our clients to update the events, therefore I decided to make it stay like that.

However, during our last presentation the clients expressed that they don’t like the agenda page and wanted more a modern vibe so for me it was such a shame because I didn’t got the chance to ask for some feedback. So next time I want to make sure that I communicate with the clients first before making anything.
